Expectation Settings

Project Length

This project is set to be for 1 (one) week period.

Nationality and Ethnicities

Your OC has to be American-blooded. This refers to at least one parent of U.S. nationality by birth.

Arcane Elements

The narrative world is set in a non-fantastical version of Earth. No magic systems, divine powers, mythological species, or metaphysical alterations are permitted as literal elements in the story. Symbolism, metaphor, philosophical hallucination, delusional perception, and unreliable narration are acceptable narrative devices but they must remain grounded in psychological, sociological, or logic.

Age Requirements

All characters and their selected faceclaims must be at least 18 years of age at the time of narrative entry. The current in-universe year is 2025, therefore you must have a birthdate prior to July 2007.

Staying Respectful

Consider your application if you are sensitive to: violent (both explicitly and implicitly), psychological horror, death, and other possible offensive topics.

Relations

Characters are permitted to form any relation within the Ordo society. Siblings, estranged parents, disappeared lovers. However, these connections must remain coherent and in character within the background structure of Ordo. No relations may contradict the foundational world logic or timeline integrity.

Creative Pursuit

You are strongly encouraged to be creative. The Ordo framework is designed to experimentation and deeply personal storytelling.

Regulations

Your character submission must follow all parameters listed in the Expectation Settings, including age, nationality, realism clause, and creative alignment.

The following RP formats are permitted under Ordo jurisdiction: GENRP (General Roleplay), OCRP (Original Character Roleplay), MVRP (Multiverse Roleplay)

One account per soul.

English is the primary and mandatory language for communication and creative entries within Ordo and Paralogism Project.

If you're allergic to reading, this is not the place for you.

NSFW parameters: Violence, gore, trauma, and psychological disintegration are permitted. However, nudity and sexual content are strictly prohibited under Ordo's policies.

Inside jokes are considered as irrelevant chatter therefore not permitted.

Activity and commitment are the bare minimum.

If you are active in other communities or RP agencies, you are free to maintain them, but you are expected to treat Ordo with equal priority.
